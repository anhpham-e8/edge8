## Dave's Story

Over the years, Dave has:

- **Automated processes at Microsoft** that moved millions of rows of data and managed millions of dollars in transactions.

- **Founded Vinasource**, a company that built web, iOS, and Android applications for clients such as the USTA (United States Tennis Association), NCAA Tennis, MTV, and Microsoft.

- **Co-founded TINYpulse**, a venture-funded startup that designed employee engagement programs for Fidelity, Sands Casino, the Boston Red Sox, and thousands of others. TINYpulse was recently acquired by WebMD.

- **Founded the Vietnam chapter of the Entrepreneurs' Organization (EO)** and currently serves as a Southeast Asia Area Director, supporting over 500 businesses that generate between $8 million and $100 million annually.
