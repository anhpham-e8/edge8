# Angi Hurt
## Building the Midwest's Strategic Nexus
### Networking and Growth Through Personal Branding

**Client:** Angi Hurt
**Category:** Personal Brand
**Website:** [angihurt.com](https://www.angihurt.com/)

---

## Case Study Summary

Angi Hurt's personal brand website transforms her reputation for connecting business leaders into a central hub for collaboration, mentorship, and growth. By weaving her small-town authenticity with corporate expertise, the platform elevates her visibility and strengthens her role as a catalyst for Midwest business innovation.

---

## The Challenge

- Needed a cohesive online presence to connect her diverse professional activities
- Wanted to expand influence beyond local circles without losing regional authenticity
- No formal platform to highlight her track record, partnerships, and insights
- Required a scalable system for sharing updates, articles, and opportunities

---

## Our Approach

- Developed a personal brand website blending Midwest values with a polished, professional aesthetic
- Organized content into Partnership Stories, Leadership Insights, and Growth Opportunities
- Created a blog strategy targeting collaboration, networking, and regional growth keywords
- Integrated AI-driven publishing for regular LinkedIn and newsletter content
- Designed a partnership inquiry form and event calendar to encourage engagement

---

## The Result

- A credible, accessible platform positioning Angi as a go-to regional connector
- Strengthened brand recognition across multiple Midwest markets
- Scalable content and events calendar that keeps her network engaged year-round
- New partnership inquiries generated within weeks of launch
