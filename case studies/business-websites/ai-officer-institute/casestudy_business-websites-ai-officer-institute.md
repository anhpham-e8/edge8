# AI Officer Institute
## Launching an AI Certification Ecosystem
### Brand, Platform, and Community in One

**Client:** AI Officer Institute
**Category:** AI Program

---

## Case Study Summary

The AI Officer Institute was created to meet the rising demand for professionals who can lead AI inside organizations. Edge8 developed the full go-to-market stack: from naming, branding, and website development to SEO content, certification logic, and launch strategy.

---

## The Challenge

- New, undefined role in the market: "AI Officer"
- Needed a trusted and professional brand presence
- Required educational credibility while feeling startup-agile
- Circle community and certification stack needed to integrate seamlessly

---

## Our Approach

- Created a full brand identity and purpose framework based on founder interviews
- Built a responsive website with SEO-rich content, landing pages, and blog
- Developed a multi-level certification system aligned with business use cases
- Designed Circle community onboarding and content experience
- Planned an 8-day launch calendar with social, email, and event strategies

---

## The Result

- Launch-ready platform with clear positioning for business leaders and professionals
- Scalable community + certification engine
- Trusted voice in the AI education ecosystem
- Official launch executed with a structured growth roadmap
