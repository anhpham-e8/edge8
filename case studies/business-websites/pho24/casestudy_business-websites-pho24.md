# PHO24
## Redesigning PHO24® for Global Reach & Franchise Growth
### Fast, SEO-Friendly, and Culturally Rooted

**Client:** PHO24®
**Category:** Business Website

---

## Case Study Summary

PHO24® is Vietnam's most iconic pho restaurant brand, with over 20 years of heritage and global recognition. As the company pursued international expansion, it needed a digital foundation to support franchise growth and brand storytelling—balancing speed, search optimization, and cultural authenticity.

---

## The Challenge

- Outdated website design not aligned with their global positioning
- Poor SEO performance with limited content structure
- No franchise funnel or strategy to capture new leads
- Sluggish page speed and mobile UX gaps

---

## Our Approach

- Designed a clean, modern interface aligned with PHO24®'s evolving brand identity
- Rebuilt the SEO structure from the ground up, including content library, internal linking, and metadata
- Created new content blocks focused on cultural insights, recipes, and franchise pathways
- Optimized performance and responsiveness across all devices

---

## The Result

- A fast, scalable, and SEO-optimized website that feels both authentic and global
- Clear CTAs and content flows that engage pho lovers and franchise partners alike
- Strong foundations for future international growth and digital content expansion
