# Nhau Vodka
## Launching Vietnam's First Story-Driven Vodka Brand
### From Culture to Clicks with SEO and Squarespace

**Client:** Alchemy Asia / Nhậu Vodka
**Category:** Business Website

---

## Case Study Summary

Alchemy Asia partnered with Edge8 to launch Nhậu Vodka—Vietnam's first rice vodka inspired by nhậu culture. The project needed to translate cultural pride into a modern brand narrative while complying with strict regulations on alcohol marketing.

---

## The Challenge

- No direct sales allowed due to alcohol regulations
- Needed to educate a younger generation on "nhậu" culture without sounding old-fashioned
- Required strong SEO visibility in a highly restricted advertising category
- Platform had to run on Squarespace per client request, limiting backend customization

---

## Our Approach

- Built a sleek, regulation-compliant Squarespace website with custom-coded popup age gate
- Developed a storytelling-first architecture with editorial-style navigation: Vodka, Cocktails, Nhậu Tales, Where To Nhậu, Buy Nhậu
- Created content pillars rooted in Vietnamese lifestyle and rituals:
  - "How to Nhậu" (social drinking etiquette)
  - "Tâm Sự" (emotional reflections over drinks)
  - "Craftsmanship" (behind the product)
- Used prompt engineering to build an AI-powered content system for future blog and social posts
- Implemented a social UGC campaign around #MyNhậuMoment to promote organic brand engagement

---

## The Result

- A visually rich brand experience blending culture, community, and compliance
- Organic SEO visibility built on brand storytelling instead of paid ads
- Culturally nuanced site that resonates with Gen Z and millennial audiences
- Strategic launchpad for future product extensions and campaigns
