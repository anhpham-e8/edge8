# InvestMigrate
## Building a Trusted EB-3 Page That Converts
### Localized Authority Page for Visa Leads in Nigeria

**Client:** InvestMigrate
**Category:** Business Website

---

## Case Study Summary

InvestMigrate is a U.S.-based immigration consultancy helping skilled professionals navigate visa options like EB-3. To build trust with Nigerian audiences, they needed a high-performance content funnel tailored to the needs, search behavior, and skepticism of the local market.

---

## The Challenge

- No dedicated entry point for Nigerian EB-3 visa applicants
- Scattered messaging and low SEO performance for relevant search terms
- Lack of trust signals for an audience wary of scams
- No content funnel guiding users from search to consultation

---

## Our Approach

- Designed and built a dedicated EB-3 visa authority page targeting Nigerian professionals
- Integrated success stories, visual trust cues, and localized messaging
- Optimized for search queries like "EB-3 visa Nigeria" and "U.S. work visa for unskilled workers"
- Built a supporting internal linking structure from blog content to strengthen SEO

---

## The Result

- A high-converting landing page that acts as the digital center of the EB-3 funnel
- Clearer, more confident positioning for Nigerian professionals and families
- Stronger lead quality and improved consultation-to-application conversion rate
