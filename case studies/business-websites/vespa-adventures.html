About the Project

Vespa Adventures

The Challenge

Vespa Adventures had a loyal audience and unique product — authentic Vespa tours through Vietnam — but their website wasn't converting curious visitors into paying customers. Booking was disconnected from content, and there was no CRM to follow up with leads.

The Solution

Edge8 redesigned the site architecture to create a seamless content-to-conversion flow. AI-powered blog content drives organic traffic, internal links guide visitors to booking pages, and a CRM integration tracks and follows up on every lead automatically.

Highlights · Booking CRM sync, blog-to-booking flow, internal linking strategy
  • CRM integration with automated lead follow-up
  • Blog-to-booking content funnels
  • Internal linking strategy for SEO and conversions
  • AI-optimized travel content for GEO discovery
  • Booking flow redesign reducing friction
