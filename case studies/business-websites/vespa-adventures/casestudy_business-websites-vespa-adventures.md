# Vespa Adventures
## Rebuild Their Digital Experience to Drive Growth
### From Generic Ecommerce to Purposeful Design

**Client:** Vespa Adventures
**Category:** Business Website
**Website:** [vespasaigon.com](https://www.vespasaigon.com/)

---

## Case Study Summary

Vespa Adventures is Southeast Asia's leading Vespa tour operator, known for immersive local experiences across Vietnam, Cambodia, and Thailand. The company needed a website that not only showcased their personality and cultural experiences, but also captured, nurtured, and converted interest from global travelers into real bookings.

---

## The Challenge

- An outdated ecommerce site that didn't reflect "The Ride of Your Life" brand promise
- Manual, disconnected booking processes
- No structure to support discovery-phase travelers
- No SEO or authority content strategy to capture high-intent traffic

---

## Our Approach

- Built a fully integrated booking + CRM system
- Developed authority pages aligned with top search intents (e.g., "Things to Do in Hanoi")
- Created blog-driven funnels to guide discovery → interest → booking
- Launched lead magnets and nurture flows for better conversion rates

---

## The Result

- A system that captures travelers earlier in their planning process
- Automated lead flows and CRM integration for better visibility and tracking
- A site built for discovery, booking, and long-term brand engagement
- 10,000 to 18,000 indexed words, signaling strong SEO content growth
- **135% increase in organic traffic year-over-year**
- Hanoi tour sales increased by **35%**, with more travelers converting from blog traffic to booked tours
