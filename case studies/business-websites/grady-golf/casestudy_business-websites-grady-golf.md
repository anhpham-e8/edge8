# Grady Golf
## Building a Beginner-First Golf Brand
### E-Commerce Meets Education with AI Support

**Client:** Grady Golf
**Category:** Business Website

---

## Case Study Summary

Grady Golf is a new golf brand that aims to make the sport more accessible and less intimidating for first-time players. They needed a website that could educate, inspire, and convert—all in one place—without overwhelming beginners with technical jargon or high-pressure sales.

---

## The Challenge

- Golf perceived as intimidating, exclusive, and complicated for beginners
- No structure for combining education and ecommerce
- Needed a tone-consistent, beginner-friendly brand identity
- Limited team capacity to scale content across blog, social, and product

---

## Our Approach

- Built a dual-path Wix website that balances "Learn" and "Shop" journeys
- Created the Grady Learning Lab: 17-part educational flow with a 90-day roadmap
- Developed a branded AI prompt engine to generate content at scale
- Implemented referral-ready ecommerce checkout and product system
- Conducted SEO audits and built blog content pillars aligned with long-tail search behavior

---

## The Result

- A scroll-friendly, clean, and warm digital brand tailored to new golfers
- A structured journey that helps first-timers learn, shop, and build confidence
- SEO-ready content system and blog hub built for discoverability and growth
- Brand identity and voice system ready for future campaigns and partnerships
