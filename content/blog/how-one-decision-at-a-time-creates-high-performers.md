## The High-Performance Decision Architecture

High performers employ a systematic five-step decision framework that eliminates cognitive overload while amplifying strategic impact:

1. **Identify your energy peaks** – Protect your naturally alert hours for your most important strategic work.
2. **Choose your "domino decision"** – Select one consistent choice that improves everything downstream.
3. **Automate the mundane** – Eliminate brainpower waste on trivial choices through meal prep, outfit planning, and scheduled workflows.
4. **Build decision-making triggers** – Create "if-then" scenarios like "If it's Monday, then I review strategic priorities first."
5. **Celebrate small wins** – Recognize each good decision as a victory; consistency beats intensity.

Strategic decision making frameworks used by high performers preserve cognitive energy for complex business challenges through systematic automation of routine choices.
